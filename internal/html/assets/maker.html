How does this work?

Creating bundles (this page)

  • Add the files you want to protect and choose who to trust
  • Set how many friends must agree (e.g., 3 of 5)
  • Generate a unique bundle for each friend
  • Send each friend their bundle (email, USB, etc.)

Recovering files (when needed)

  • Friends open their bundle's recover.html in any browser
  • They combine their pieces (enough must agree)
  • Files are decrypted and recovered ‚Äî no server needed

Security notes

  • Everything runs in your browser ‚Äî nothing leaves your device
  • This page can be saved and used offline
  • Files are encrypted with age
  • Open source (Apache-2.0)
